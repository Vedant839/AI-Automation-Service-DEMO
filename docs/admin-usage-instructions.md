# Admin Usage Instructions

## Support Assistant

- Update FAQ and course content only after internal approval.
- Review unresolved questions daily during launch week.
- Escalate policy exceptions to counselors.
- Do not paste passwords, OTPs, government IDs, banking details, or full payment records into assistant tools.

## Engagement Automation

- Review counselor alerts daily.
- Confirm parent notifications before enabling automated sends in production.
- Update thresholds only after approval from academic/support leadership.
- Track false positives and false negatives during the first two weeks.

## Teacher Reports

- Confirm recipient list before launch.
- Review report quality weekly.
- Use weak-topic trends to plan class recaps.
- Avoid forwarding reports outside approved staff groups.

## Maintenance

Included monthly maintenance generally covers monitoring, prompt optimization, minor workflow updates, and bug fixes. Major new features should go through the change-request process.
