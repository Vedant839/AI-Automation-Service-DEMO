# Acceptance Checklist

## AI Support Assistant

- Answers approved FAQs correctly
- Escalates sensitive or low-confidence queries
- Works in approved MVP channels
- Logs only approved data

## Engagement Automation

- Detects inactive students
- Detects low-performing students
- Detects missed classes
- Detects score decline
- Sends or stages parent notifications as approved
- Creates counselor escalation alerts

## Teacher Reports

- Generates daily summary
- Includes weak topics
- Includes frequent doubts
- Includes at-risk student count
- Uses approved privacy settings
- Delivers to approved recipients

## Deployment

- Production credentials configured securely
- Monitoring enabled
- Staff onboarding completed
- Rollback path documented
- Known limitations acknowledged
