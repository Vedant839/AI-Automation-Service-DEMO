# AI Automation Services Agreement (Mock Contract)

## Between

### Client

BrightPath Learning Pvt. Ltd.
(“Client”)

AND

### Service Provider

Vedant Automation Solutions
(“Consultant”)

Effective Date: 15 June 2026

---

# 1. Project Overview

The Consultant agrees to design and implement AI-powered workflow automation systems for the Client’s education platform.

Primary goals:

* Reduce student support response time
* Automate repetitive counselor interactions
* Improve student engagement tracking
* Generate teacher analytics summaries
* Improve operational efficiency

---

# 2. Scope of Work

The Consultant shall provide the following services:

## A. AI Support Assistant

Development of AI-powered support systems capable of:

* Answering course-related FAQs
* Responding to common student doubts
* Providing batch and scheduling information
* Handling scholarship and fee-related queries

Deployment channels:

* Website chat widget
* Mobile application chat
* WhatsApp integration

---

## B. Student Engagement Automation

Automation workflows for:

* Inactive students
* Low-performing students
* Missed classes
* Test score decline
* Parent notification triggers
* Counselor escalation alerts

---

## C. Teacher Intelligence Dashboard

Automated daily summaries including:

* Weak chapters/topics
* Frequently asked doubts
* At-risk students
* Engagement analytics

Delivery frequency:

* Daily evening reports

---

# 3. Project Phases

## Phase 1 — Discovery & Workflow Analysis

Duration: 7 days

Includes:

* Workflow mapping
* CRM analysis
* Data structure review
* Risk identification
* Integration feasibility assessment

---

## Phase 2 — MVP Development

Duration: 30 days

Includes:

* FAQ automation
* Basic engagement workflows
* Initial dashboard
* Internal testing

---

## Phase 3 — Deployment & Monitoring

Duration: 8 days

Includes:

* Production deployment
* Staff onboarding
* Monitoring setup
* Bug fixing
* Performance validation

---

# 4. Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to provide:

* Access to relevant APIs
* Course documentation
* FAQ documents
* CRM access (limited as required)
* Test/staging environment access
* Technical point of contact

The Client remains responsible for:

* Data legality
* Student consent compliance
* Internal policy approvals

---

# 5. Data & Privacy

## Data Access

The Consultant shall only access data necessary for project functionality.

Sensitive information including:

* payment records,
* government IDs,
* passwords,
* banking information

shall not be shared unless explicitly required.

---

## Confidentiality

The Consultant agrees to:

* Keep all client data confidential
* Not share data with third parties
* Use secured authentication practices
* Limit internal data exposure

---

## AI Provider Usage

The Client acknowledges that third-party AI APIs may process limited query data for functionality purposes.

The Consultant shall use commercially reasonable efforts to:

* minimize exposure,
* anonymize data where possible,
* disable unnecessary logging.

---

# 6. Deliverables

The Consultant shall deliver:

* AI chatbot workflows
* Engagement automation system
* Teacher reporting system
* Deployment documentation
* Admin usage instructions

---

# 7. Exclusions (Out of Scope)

The following are NOT included in the initial agreement:

* AI voice calling systems
* Full custom CRM rebuild
* Native mobile app development
* Manual content moderation
* Human customer support staffing
* Advanced multilingual fine-tuning
* Real-time live tutoring AI

Additional features may require:

* revised pricing,
* timeline extension,
* separate agreement.

---

# 8. Performance Expectations

The Consultant does not guarantee:

* 100% AI accuracy
* zero hallucinations
* uninterrupted third-party API availability

The Consultant shall make commercially reasonable efforts to maintain:

* high uptime,
* response quality,
* workflow reliability.

---

₹6,00,000

Payment schedule:

* 40% upfront

* 20% after deployment

---

## Monthly Maintenance

₹80,000/month

Excludes major feature additions.

---

# 10. Intellectual Property

Custom workflows, prompts, automation logic, and deployment configurations created specifically for the Client shall become Client property after full payment.

Pre-existing frameworks, reusable tooling, and internal utilities remain Consultant property.

---

# 11. Limitation of Liability

The Consultant shall not be liable for:

* indirect damages,
* revenue loss,
* data issues originating from client systems,
* third-party outages,
* misuse of AI-generated outputs.

Total liability shall not exceed the total amount paid under this agreement.

---

# 12. Change Requests

Any major feature request outside defined scope, including but not limited to:

* AI voice agents,
* multilingual expansion,
* new platform integrations,
* custom analytics modules,

shall require:

* written approval,
* revised timelines,
* updated pricing.

---

# 13. Termination

Either party may terminate the agreement with 15 days written notice.

The Client shall pay for completed work up to termination date.

Upon termination:

* access credentials shall be revoked,
* confidential data shall be deleted where applicable.
