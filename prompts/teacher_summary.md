# Teacher Summary Prompt Template

Create a daily teacher intelligence summary for {{client_name}}.

## Include

- Top weak chapters/topics
- Frequently asked doubts
- At-risk student count
- Recommended class-level actions
- Recommended individual follow-ups where permitted

## Rules

- Do not expose unnecessary personal data.
- Use student IDs instead of names unless client policy permits names.
- Do not include payment, password, government ID, or banking information.
- Separate observed facts from recommendations.
